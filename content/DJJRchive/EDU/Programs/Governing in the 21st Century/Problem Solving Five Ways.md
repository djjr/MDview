# Problem Solving Five Ways
## rational, creative, engineering, legal, political
Explores five distinct professional approaches to tackling complex challenges: rational analysis, creative innovation, engineering pragmatism, legal reasoning, and political negotiation. Students will learn how each mode structures problems, defines constraints, and evaluates success, gaining practical insight into when and how to apply each. By the end of the course, they will not only have a flexible problem-solving toolkit but also the ability to collaborate across disciplines and approach challenges from multiple perspectives.
