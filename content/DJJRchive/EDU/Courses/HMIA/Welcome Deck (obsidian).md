## Why are we here? What is alignment? 
</grid>

<iframe width="600" height="400" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/WvmeTaFc_Qw?si=UviXs4YVPqwQnRBC" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>
2015 5m18s
note:
###### Takeaways
1. success depends on aligning machine objectives with human preferences (1:54–2:08).
2. specifying objectives well is hard. It remains a serious risk. (2:11–2:50).
3. If super-intelligence, critical that their values compatible with human to prevent catastrophe (3:04–3:11).
4. Value alignment is like containment in nuclear technology. Solving alignment needs to be a priority of research not just an ancillary concern. (3:35–3:49).
5. Russell suggests that 'inverse reinforcement learning'—where machines observe and learn human preferences from our actions and history—alongside an ongoing, deep conversation about human values, is essential for designing beneficial super-intelligent systems (3:53–4:48).

## _Further Reading on Alignment_

  - <span style="font-size: 0.75em;">IBM: ["What is alignment?"](https://research.ibm.com/blog/what-is-alignment-ai). General answer with examples of things IBM is developing</span>
   - <span style="font-size: 0.75em;">Wikipedia. ["Al Alignment"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_alignment) 25-30 min read
      Read first three sections and skim the rest for a first reading.</span>
  - <span style="font-size: 0.75em;">Vinod Chugani 2024: ["What is AI Alignment? Ensuring AI Works for Humanity"](https://www.datacamp.com/blog/ai-alignment) on DataCamp blog. · 12 min read. Ignore opening idea (building in values); after that several good break downs of concepts related to alignment.</span>
  - <span style="font-size: 0.75em;">Paul Christiano. 2018. ["Clarifying 'AI alignment'".](https://ai-alignment.com/clarifying-ai-alignment-cec47cd69dd6) 4 min read. Head of AI safety at the US AI Safety Institute</span>
  - <span style="font-size: 0.75em;">Ji, J. et al. 2024. ["AI Alignment: A Comprehensive Survey"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.19852) 60PP! Dense text, read from the outside in - look at contents and overall shape.</span>
  - <span style="font-size: 0.75em;">Tech AI Digest. 2024. "[AI Alignment Explained: Ensuring AI Follows Human Values](https://youtu.be/MTia_5C5aHI?si=_p-xFo4n6zbix1T2)" (11min podcast). 2 person conversation - very beginner friendly.
